Our Features

A group of foodies came together who are really passionate about the food. We offer a variety of features to help you manage your recipes and shopping list, but doing it in a really fashionable way and most of all - it's really easy to use.

  • edit_note Add, remove or manage your recipes (and also keep track of calories)
  • list_alt Manage your shopping list to keep track of what you need
  • group Collaborate with others who are passionate about food (food blogging, food photography, discovering new places etc)
